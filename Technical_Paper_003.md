Koki's Technical Paper #003

Why I think CASB is Essential

Today, I focused on CASB. To me, it feels like a "Universal Remote Control" for cloud security.

In a world where everyone uses different clouds, we need one central point to see everything. Without CASB, we are blind to where our data goes. By mastering this, I want to ensure that no matter where data travels, it remains under our protection.
