 Mastering Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) Concepts
 
In cybersecurity, visibility is power. Today, I explored SIEM (Security Information and Event Management), a vital tool for any Security Analyst.

Think of SIEM as the "Brain" of a security operation. It collects logs from every corner of the network—firewalls, servers, and tripwires—and analyzes them in real-time. By correlating different events, SIEM helps us spot complex attacks that might look like normal activity on a single device.

Mastering SIEM is essential for effective incident response and proactive defense.
