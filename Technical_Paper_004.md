Koki's Technical Paper #004
My Perspective on Vulnerability Management
I learned that security isn't about being perfect; it’s about Vulnerability Management.
We cannot fix everything at once. That’s why we use CVSS scores. It’s like a doctor triage—we treat the most "bleeding" wounds first. For me, understanding these scores is the key to staying calm during a cyber attack. We don't panic; we just follow the numbers and fix the biggest holes.
