Koki's Technical Paper #011

Discerning the "Gravity" of Information : My Defense Against Fake News

1. What is "Information Gravity"?
I have learned that information has weight. I call it "Gravity."
True facts are heavy because they are solid. They don't change even when you push them.
But Fake News is different. It is like weightless dust floating in the air. It looks shiny and moves fast, but it has no substance. I must touch the information and feel if it is heavy or light.

2. Why do Lies move so fast?
Mentors taught me that lies use a "Force" called emotion.
When I see something that makes me suddenly scared or very angry, I must stop. That is the acceleration of a lie. It is trying to hijack my mind. If I feel a sudden "pull" in my heart, I will call it "Malicious Acceleration" and wait before I believe it.

3. My Verification Protocol
To protect my dignity, I will follow these steps :
・ Checking the Source : Who sent this? Is there a reason they want me to feel scared?
・Wait and See : Lies dissipate like fog. Truth stays on the ground. I will wait for the information to "settle."
・Logical Friction : I will rub this information against what I already know is true. If it breaks, it is a lie.

Conclusion
I will not be a leaf blown by the wind of the internet. I will be a stone with my own "Gravity." By thinking for myself, I remain the architect of my own world.
